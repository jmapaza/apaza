arequipa dance

Arequipa is one of the cities that live with great enthusiasm the carnival party between February and March. Cheerful troupes composed of young dancers dance non-stop through the main squares and streets of the "White City". Carnival is said to be a dance performed in honor of the god Momo.

zamacueca dance
